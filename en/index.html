Association Introduction

The Hong Kong Thangka Art Association (HKTAA) is a non-governmental organization established with approval from the Hong Kong SAR Government. The association is dedicated to promoting the inheritance and development of Thangka art, providing a global platform for cultural exchange in Thangka art. It brings together Thangka art enthusiasts, scholars, and artists from around the world. Through exhibitions, lectures, workshops, and academic exchange activities, the association aims to enhance understanding and appreciation of Thangka art in various communities.

Thangka, as an art form deeply rooted in religious culture, not only carries rich artistic value but also embodies the spiritual essence of Buddhist culture.

The mission of the association is to promote the internationalization of Thangka art, encouraging more people to understand and respect this traditional art form, while providing contemporary Thangka artists with a platform for exchange and exhibition. We believe that through various activities and projects, Thangka art will receive greater attention and recognition globally, and will find more development opportunities by integrating tradition and innovation.

The Hong Kong Thangka Art Association warmly invites anyone with an interest in Thangka art to join us in advancing and promoting this ancient art form.
